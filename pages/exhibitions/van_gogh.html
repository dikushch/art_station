Van Gogh. Letters to Theo

5 JUNE - 10 SEPTEMBER

Van Gogh is one of the most popular artists in the world. During his short creative life, the master has created more than 2,000 works, including 930 paintings, 1,100 drawings and sketches. His creations are traditionally the most expensive and desirable lots in any auction.

Created especially for the Van Gogh Year, an updated version of the Van Gogh Alive exhibition gives a new glance on the tragic life and the magnificent work of the artist. 400 most iconic paintings by Van Gogh, great classical music, the huge screens on the total exhibition area of over 2,000 square meters will undoubtedly make a lasting impression on the audience.
